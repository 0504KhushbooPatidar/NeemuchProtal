Businesses in Neemuch

Explore the major business and trade activities of Neemuch district

Opium Trade

Neemuch is world famous for its **Opium (Afeem) trade**. It houses the **Government Opium & Alkaloid Factory**, which is one of the largest legal opium factories in the world. Farmers in the region cultivate opium under government license, making it a significant part of the district’s economy and identity.

Soyabean Market

Soyabean is another major agricultural product of Neemuch. The **Neemuch Mandi** is a hub for soyabean trade and is considered one of the largest grain markets in Asia. Soyabean from Neemuch is known for its **quality and high yield**, playing a vital role in the region’s agricultural economy.
